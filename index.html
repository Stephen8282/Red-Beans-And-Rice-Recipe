Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Ingredients Instructions Tips

Ingredients

  • 1 lb red beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb smoked sausage, sliced
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked white rice

Instructions

  1. Soak the beans overnight in water.
  2. In a large pot, sauté onion, bell pepper, and garlic.
  3. Add the sliced sausage and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Drain and rinse the soaked beans, then add them to the pot.
  5. Pour in water, add bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve over cooked white rice and enjoy!

Tips

For extra flavor, consider adding a smoked ham hock or Andouille sausage.
